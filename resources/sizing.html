Our caps include elastic in the rear and are one-size-fits-most. We also make a large size for those of us who are cranially gifted, but we realize that many people don't fit into the standard sizing so if you require a specially sized cap, feel free to contact us and we'll make sure you get a cap you'll be happy with.

This isn't an exact science, but here's a general sizing chart to get you in the ballpark. To measure your head, grab a tape measure (the flexible kind, not metal) or a string which you'll measure later and wrap it around your noggin, lining it up where the sweatband of a cap would sit.

Cognition Size Inches Hat Size
One Size Fits Most 22 to 23-1/2 7 to 7-1/2
Large 23 to 24-5/8 7-3/8 to 7-7/8
